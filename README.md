## 🧠 About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at **Technische Universität Darmstadt** with hands-on experience building real-time AI and computer vision systems.

Most recently I co-founded **FLARE** — a student engineering project at TU Darmstadt — where I led the development of a YOLO-based wildfire detection pipeline deployed on a Raspberry Pi 5 with an AI HAT+ for autonomous UAV-based field monitoring. I also competed with the team at **XtraChallenge 2025**, a national UAV design and flight competition at Universitat Politècnica de València, Spain 🇪🇸.

I focus on applying AI to real-world, physical problems — not just notebooks.
